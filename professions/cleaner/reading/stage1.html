Stage 1 - Phone Screening Interview

Improve your English by reading real-life interview scenarios and answering questions to test your understanding. Build confidence with instant feedback and practical vocabulary.

Hi, is this Maria?

Yes, speaking.

Hi Maria, this is Emily from Spark Cleaning Services. Is now a good time for a short phone interview?

Yes, of course.

Great. Can you tell me about your experience in cleaning?

I’ve worked in the UK and India for 6 years. Mostly in offices, private homes, and medical clinics.

What type of cleaning tasks are you most comfortable with?

Dusting, mopping, disinfecting surfaces, bathroom sanitation — general and deep cleaning.

Do you have any local certificates or WHMIS training?

No, not yet, but I’m open to training.

Are you able to commute between different job locations?

Yes, I have my own transportation.

Would you be open to early morning or late evening shifts?

Yes, that’s not a problem.

How soon could you start if selected?

Right away. I’m fully available.

Thanks Maria. We’ll be in touch soon.

Thank you. I’m looking forward to working with your team.
