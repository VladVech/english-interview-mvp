Cleaning Specialist - Listening

Stage 1 - Phone Screening Interview

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. Where has Maria worked as a cleaner before?




  2. What places has she cleaned?




  3. Which cleaning tasks is Maria most comfortable with?




  4. Does she have any cleaning-related certificates?




  5. How does Maria commute between job sites?




  6. Is she open to early or late shifts?




  7. When is Maria available to start?




  8. How does Maria feel about joining the team?
