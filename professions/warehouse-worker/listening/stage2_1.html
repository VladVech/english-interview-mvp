Warehouse Worker - Listening

Stage 2_1 - Final Interview Round

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. What kind of warehouse has Natalia worked in before?




  2. What tasks did she perform at her previous job?




  3. Has Natalia used pallet jacks before?




  4. What is she open to training on?




  5. Has she worked in a cold or loud environment?




  6. How does she manage cold conditions?




  7. What tools does she use to ensure accuracy?




  8. What’s her method to reduce errors?
