About This Job

A Senior 3D Artist is responsible for creating high-quality 3D assets for games, films, simulations, or architectural visualizations. Typical tasks include modeling, texturing, lighting, rendering, and optimizing 3D scenes using industry-standard software such as Blender, Maya, 3ds Max, or Unreal Engine. They may also lead visual development, supervise junior artists, and ensure the visual consistency of a project.

This is an advanced creative role that requires years of experience and a strong portfolio. Employers value technical expertise, artistic vision, problem-solving skills, and the ability to work collaboratively in production pipelines. Strong English communication is important for feedback, documentation, and teamwork. This position often serves as a bridge between art direction and production.
