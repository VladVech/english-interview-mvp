About This Job

A caregiver provides assistance and support to elderly individuals or people with disabilities, helping them live safely and comfortably at home or in care facilities. Typical tasks include helping with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, and meal preparation. Caregivers may also assist with light housekeeping, medication reminders, and companionship.

This is a reliable entry-level job with high demand across North America. It does not always require certification, making it suitable for newcomers or international job seekers. Employers value patience, compassion, attention to detail, and the ability to follow care instructions. Basic English communication skills are helpful for interacting with clients and their families.
