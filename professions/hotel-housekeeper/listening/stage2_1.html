Hotel Housekeeper - Listening

Stage 2_1 - Final Interview Round

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. Why does Anna want to work at Mapleview Hotel?




  2. How did Anna handle a busy weekend with early check-ins at her previous job?




  3. What does Anna do when guests request something outside her duties?




  4. What is Anna’s view on eco-friendly cleaning?




  5. What did Anna ask at the end of the interview?




  6. How does the hotel help new employees adjust?




  7. What quality does Anna highlight about herself in the interview?




  8. What was Anna’s final comment in the interview?
