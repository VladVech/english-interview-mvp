Hotel Housekeeper - Listening

Stage 2_2 - Final Interview Round

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. What does Viktor consider essential when working with staff or guests?




  2. How did Viktor resolve a conflict about room assignments?




  3. What is Viktor’s attitude toward receiving criticism?




  4. How does Viktor manage time when several rooms must be cleaned quickly?




  5. What does Viktor do if a guest reports a missing item?




  6. What keeps Viktor motivated during long shifts?




  7. What did Viktor ask about at the end of the interview?




  8. What did the interviewer say about career development?
