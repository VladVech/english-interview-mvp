Warehouse Worker - Listening

Stage 2_2 – Final Interview Round

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. Where has Tomasz previously worked in warehouses?




  2. What were Tomasz’s typical shift responsibilities?




  3. How does Tomasz stay organized during busy shifts?




  4. How did Tomasz handle a disagreement at work?




  5. Is Tomasz open to working overtime or weekends?




  6. What tools has Tomasz used in the warehouse?




  7. Is Tomasz willing to learn new systems?




  8. When can Tomasz start the job?
