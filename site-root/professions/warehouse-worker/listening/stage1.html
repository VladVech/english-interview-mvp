Warehouse Worker - Listening

Stage 1 - Phone Screening Interview

Improve your English by listening to real spoken texts and answering questions to check your understanding. Practice at your own pace and get immediate feedback.

Listen carefully and test how well you’ve understood — with instant feedback.

  1. What countries has Daniel worked in?




  2. What kind of warehouse tasks did he perform?




  3. Does Daniel currently have a Canadian forklift license?




  4. Is he open to forklift training in Canada?




  5. What kind of equipment has Daniel used?




  6. Is Daniel open to working weekends or evenings?




  7. Does he have transportation to the warehouse?




  8. Is Daniel legally authorized to work in Canada?
