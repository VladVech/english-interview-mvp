Warehouse Worker - Listening

Stage 2_1 - Final Interview Round

1. What kind of warehouse has Natalia worked in before?




2. What tasks did she perform at her previous job?




3. What equipment has Natalia used before?




4. Is Natalia willing to learn forklift operation?




5. Has she worked in refrigerated environments?




6. How does she handle cold working conditions?




7. What method does Natalia use to stay accurate and fast?




8. When will Natalia hear back from the company?
