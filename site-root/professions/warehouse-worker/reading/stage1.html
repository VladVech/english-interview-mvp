Stage 1 - Phone Screening Interview

Improve your English by reading real-life interview scenarios and answering questions to test your understanding. Build confidence with instant feedback and practical vocabulary.

Good morning, am I speaking with Daniel?

Yes, this is Daniel.

Hi Daniel, this is Michelle calling from Swift Logistics. I hope I’m not catching you at a bad time.

No, it's a good time. Thank you.

Great. I’m reaching out regarding the warehouse associate position you applied for. This is a short screening call just to get to know your background a bit and see if this could be a good fit.

Sure, sounds good.

Can you tell me a little about your previous warehouse experience?

Yes, I’ve worked in warehouse roles for about 4 years, mostly in Poland and the UK. I handled packing, inventory, loading/unloading, and forklift operations.

Do you currently hold any certifications, like forklift operation or WHMIS?

I have a forklift license from Europe, but not a Canadian one yet. I’m open to taking the training here.

Are you comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and standing for long periods?

Yes, I’m used to physical work. It’s never been a problem.

Have you worked with inventory systems or scanners?

Yes, I used handheld scanners and basic tracking software before.

Are you open to shift work, including evenings or weekends if needed?

Yes, I’m very flexible with hours.

Do you have reliable transportation to and from the warehouse location?

Yes, I do.

And just to confirm, you’re legally authorized to work in Canada?

Yes, I hold a valid work permit.

Excellent. Based on what you’ve shared, your background sounds like it could be a great match. We'll be reviewing all candidates over the next few days.

Thank you. I appreciate the opportunity.

  2. Does Daniel have a Canadian forklift license?
  3. What kind of inventory tools has Daniel used?
