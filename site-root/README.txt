# Joblish – English for Job Interviews

**Joblish** is a learning platform designed to help newcomers, immigrants, and ESL learners prepare for English-language job interviews in North America and beyond.

## 🎯 Features

- Profession-based navigation (Warehouse Worker, Cleaner, Driver, etc.)
- Listening sections with real interview audio
- Reading comprehension based on interview scripts
- Vocabulary and grammar practice
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Color-coded roles (interviewer/candidate)

## 🌍 Who It's For

- Newcomers and immigrants to the USA, Canada, and the British Commonwealth
- ESL learners preparing for job interviews
- Students in training programs for trades and entry-level positions

## 🔧 Technologies Used

- HTML5 / CSS3 (no frameworks)
- Mobile responsive layout
- Manual folder structure per profession
- No backend (static site)
